Important Information for Parents and Students

January 2024

  1. As the winter season is in place, we would like to remind you about the traffic regulation at the Chinese School. In order to avoid congestion and accidents in the Parking Lot, please Do Not Park in the Fire Lane Zone or no parking area, violator will be ticketed from the City of Ottawa.
  2. There will be no school on
    • Family Day weekend: Feb 17, 2024
  3. The annual Chinese Lunar New Year Carnival will be held on February 3, 2024 in the Cafeteria. There will be games, prizes, treats, Lion Dance and Culture Performances to celebrate the “Year of the Dragon”. You are encouraged to wear a Chinese outﬁt for this event. Note: There will be no morning classes, please arrive at 10:00 a.m. for this fun event.
  4. After School Interest Class registration has started in room 226. The first day of Interest Class is January 20, there will be no interest class on February 10 due to the Chinese New Year.
  5. During the winter season, students are encouraged to bring in their indoor shoes for Saturday school.
  6. School Address: Notre Dame High School, 710 Broadview Ave.
    Phone Number for Saturday (Capital Chinese School): 613-293-2129

On behalf of the Capital Chinese School at Notre Dame, wishing you the best in 2024.
