秋/冬季班

时间：每年九月初至六月，周六上午9:30至中午12点。

班级：国语）幼儿园低班到12级，成人班。

下午学生课外兴趣班 (12:10-1:10pm)：歌舞，数学，算盘，中国象棋，围棋 武术, 电子琴，中国节编织，绘画。

家长室内活动包括：篮球和羽毛球等。

暑期班

时间：每年七月至八月，周一至周五(半日或全日)。

Fall/Winter Term

Time: Beginning of September to June Saturdays, 9:30am to 12:00pm

Class: (simplified Mandarin) A total of 14 levels from Junior Kindergarden to level 12, Adult Class

Parents recreational activities: Basketball, Badminton and so on.

Afternoon extra activities: Singing and Dancing, Mathematics, Abacus, Chinese Chess, Go, Kungfu, Electronic Keyboard, Chinese Knot Weaving and Drawing

Summer School

Time: July to August Weekdays, Half or Full Day
